Pokemon

Pokemon is a Japanese adventure turn-based role-playing game that a lot of people love to play. You get to battle gym leaders and find new pokemon to befriend and fight alongside you. Pokemon in Japanese is referred to as ポケモン or Poketto Monsutā1. The first game, Pokemon Red and Pokemon Green was launched on Febuary 27th, 1996.

The creator of Pokemon is Satoshi Tajiri. He was born on August 28, 1965 in Machida, Tokyo, Japan.

Gym Leaders

There are 8 gym leaders that can be found in any city within each Pokemon game.
Their pokemons level and amount of pokemon increases by how much gym leaders you have beaten. They typically have around 4-5 pokemon at the last gym.
